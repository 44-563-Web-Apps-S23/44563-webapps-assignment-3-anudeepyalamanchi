Navy blue Yak

Navy blue Yaks are superheroes which are heavily built animals with bulky frames, sturdy legs, rounded, cloven hooves, and extremely dense, long fur that hangs down lower than the belly. It has Super Strength to defeat the enemies and saves the people from danger. It has Super Speed with fast healing body and emit laser beams from his eyes. No one could see the eyes directly, because if they saw him, his eyes immediately shot lasers.

  • Super strength
  • Super speed
  • Fast healing
  • Laser eyes
